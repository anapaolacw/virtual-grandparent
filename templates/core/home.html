About us

Virtual Grandparent is a multipurpose online project to help connect helpers with the older generation, by specifying a purpose of connection.

This projects aims to improve the quality of life of older generations through various means of support, including home visits, social activities, technology support, transportation, and much much more.

"Giving back is as good for you as it is for those you are helping, because giving gives you purpose. When you have a purpose-driven life, you’re a happier person" — Goldie Hawn
